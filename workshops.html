Radical Ecologies

Prinzessinnengärten, Berlin, Friday 28th September 2-5pm

Register now for free and secure your place in the workshop!

Our first workshop Radical Ecologies will be held as part of Retune Festival 2018. The workshop is the first in a series of workshops by Radical Spaces Laboratory which uses participatory design as a method to engage participants to think critically with systems of scale and imagine new alternatives. We will explore questions such as: How can design engage with complex spaces in meaningful ways? How can we experiment with new strategies, systems, technologies, topologies and politics creating new ways of living and experiencing? What types of tools can we employ to engage with these systems of scale the the environments they produce?

The workshop will use methods from systems analysis, critical thinking and speculative design to deconstruct and reimagine complex environments investigating system layers and co-designing responses. Participants will explore system topologies, discuss and map the different actors that are at play, creating experimental narratives and prototyping artifacts/scenarios that explore new relationships and connections. The workshop structure is focused on co-creation and group dialogue rather than a learning curriculum or lecture and is open for participation to anyone with no necessary experience or background in design. How to get to the workshop: The workshop is located at Prinzessinnengärten Berlin.

Aims of the Workshop:

  • Use design methods to explore our ecosystems (political, cultural, environmental, technological).
  • To engage with critical design methods and new ways of thinking.
  • To foster an environment of co-design, working together.
  • Plant ideas for scaffolding further collaboration/thoughts/projects.

Workshop Agenda:

  • Introduction to the Radical Spaces Lab project
  • Deconstructing systems:
    • Mapping actors, forces and spaces
    • Analysing system relationships
  • Speculating alternatives:
    • Creating new narratives
    • Prototyping our ideas
  • Wrap up and discussing created works

Outcomes:

  • Learning to think in new design paradigms and creative ways
  • Hold a new perspective on design for transformative agency
  • Rapid prototyping explorations into large scale problems

Duration:

The workshop will last approximately 4 hours
